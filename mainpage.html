Juliana Planas

Hello, and thank you for reviewing my profile. I am a linguistics graduate with a passion for digital technology and web development. Over the past few years, I have been training in various programming languages to complement my academic background. My goal is to merge my interests in language and art with digital technology to create innovative solutions.

I thrive in collaborative environments and enjoy working with others to achieve shared goals. In addition to my professional pursuits, I am constantly exploring personal projects, learning new languages, and engaging in physical activity. I believe that continuous learning is essential to staying current in the ever-evolving field of digital technology.

In my personal life, I enjoy surrounding myself with natural beauty, such as fresh flowers in my home. I also enjoy taking care of my curly hair and reading between three and five books per month to expand my knowledge and imagination.

Events

Sepicall

Co-creator, producer, community management and moderation during the party

2020

Sepicall worked as a virtual community that looked to offer a dissemination platform for diverse artists, DJs and emerging producers who, in the context of preventive and compulsory social distancing, saw their sources of income diminished. In addition, it offered entertainment in a space of enjoyment, dance and socialization for all people, in a context that prevents enjoying the public space to socialize.

Alta Paja

Co-creator, producer, community and artist management

2017-2019

A show whose main focus was contemporary poetry, but which combined other disciplines such as music, scenic and visual with the intention of making artistic sensuality and sexual diversity visible in equal measure.

Todxs lxs chicxs

Creator, producer and community management

2017

Cultural collective composed by a diversity of artists, poets, musicians, illustrators, designers, photographers, actors and all those interested in art and who would like to spread their work.
